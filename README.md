# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze Blinkit's grocery sales performance, customer ratings, product categories, and outlet operations. This project transforms raw retail data into actionable business insights through dynamic visualizations, KPI tracking, and interactive reporting.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to provide a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales data and help stakeholders understand:

- Sales performance across outlets
- Customer purchasing patterns
- Product category performance
- Outlet location effectiveness
- Customer rating trends
- Revenue-driving business segments

Using Power BI, the dashboard converts complex retail data into an easy-to-understand decision-support system.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional data every day. Without proper analysis, identifying sales trends and growth opportunities becomes difficult.

This dashboard was built to answer key business questions such as:

- Which product categories generate the highest sales?
- Which outlet locations contribute the most revenue?
- How does outlet size impact sales performance?
- What are the customer rating trends?
- Which outlet types perform best?

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🥇 Top Product Categories

The most frequently sold product categories include:

- Fruits and Vegetables
- Snack Foods
- Household Products
- Frozen Foods
- Dairy Products

### 📍 Outlet Distribution

- Tier 3 locations contain the highest number of outlets.
- Medium-sized outlets represent the largest outlet segment.

### ⭐ Customer Satisfaction

- Average customer rating remains close to 4/5 across products.
- Consistent ratings indicate stable customer satisfaction.

### 💰 Revenue Performance

- Sales performance varies significantly across outlet types and locations.
- Certain outlet categories contribute disproportionately to overall revenue.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

### 1. Expand High-Performing Categories
Increase inventory allocation for top-selling product categories such as Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods.

### 2. Focus on High-Revenue Locations
Identify top-performing outlet locations and prioritize expansion efforts.

### 3. Improve Low-Performing Outlets
Implement targeted marketing and promotional campaigns in underperforming outlets.

### 4. Customer Retention Strategy
Maintain product quality and customer service standards to preserve strong ratings.

### 5. Data-Driven Decision Making
Use dashboard insights regularly to optimize inventory planning and outlet operations.
